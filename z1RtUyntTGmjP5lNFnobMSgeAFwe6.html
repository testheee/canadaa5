How to get rich (and not burn out)

Faustino
29.09.2021

It's a long game, both mentally and physically.It's about your health, your financial well-being and your ability to earn a lot of money. Each person has their own unique path to prosperity and financial well-being. Therefore, each person will have to take into their own hands the issue of increasing their income. In this article, we will talk about how to start your journey to higher incomes, and what steps each person will have to take to do this.At the beginning, take care of your health!This means taking care of your body and the health of your nervous system:Take care of your body: Eat well, exercise regularly, and drink plenty of fluids. Do not forget to take care of your joints and your body: maintain a healthy lifestyle, do not smoke, take care of your skin, etc. As soon as you have a "financial cushion" and you are "all set", feel free to move on to investing.To do this, it is important to have a strong "money" signal (due to the regular purchase of currency, stocks, and other financial instruments). To do this:Keep your balance of "active and passive "income. Be sure to have a spare "Tuner" for special occasions and unforeseen circumstances. Wealth is not about works. We are not talking about heavy industry and mining. We are talking about real estate, business, and the professions. Wealth is about your investment and the rotation of your money correctly:Do not be lazy to learn how to invest correctly and increase the money you earn.To do this, make a good habit of investing 10-20% of every income you earn.Be sure to use a monthly averaging strategy (and keep your balance of recent and established investments). If you still don't have a "financial cushion", then you should take the very first steps to increase your income. 1) read my article " Where NOT to invest money? TOP 3 most dangerous places for money". 2) Read my article "what skills you need to become rich".
